# Want to restrict our daily news and bot into a specific text channel? 
# Check out the following steps below!

## 1. Click on channel settings.
![channel settings](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/channel%20settings.PNG)
## 2. Switch to the permissions tab.
![permissions tab](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/permissions%20tab.PNG)

## 3. Go to "ROLES/MEMBERS" and add a new role
![ROLES/MEMBERS](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/add%20a%20new%20role.PNG)

## 4. In "ADD:", search for "The COVID-19 Info Bot", and click on it
![Add role](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/select%20role.PNG)

## 5. Scroll down until you see "TEXT PERMISSIONS" and "Send Messages" 
![TEXT PERMISSIONS](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/TEXT%20PERMISSIONS.PNG)

## 6. Disable the "Send Messages".
![Disable text permissions](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/disable.PNG)

## 7. Save your changes. 
![save changes](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/save.PNG)

## 8. Does our bot have any other roles? Repeat these steps for all the roles that the bot has!
![Other roles](https://github.com/Donald-K-Lee/TheCOVID19InfoBot.github.io/blob/master/image%20for%20tutorial/otherroles.PNG)
